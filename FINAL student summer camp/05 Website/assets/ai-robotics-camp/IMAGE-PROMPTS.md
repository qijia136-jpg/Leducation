# AI + Robotics Summer Camp — Image Generation Prompts

## Global Style Guidelines

- **Style**: Photorealistic, editorial/documentary style, warm natural lighting, vibrant but natural colors
- **Subjects**: Teenagers aged 14–18, racially diverse group in every image (include East Asian, South Asian, Black, White, Latino/Hispanic, Middle Eastern representation across the set)
- **Setting**: Modern Chinese campus/university environment with subtle AI/robotics context
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (1920×1080) or 4:3 (1600×1200)
- **Mood**: Energetic, curious, collaborative, warm, aspirational
- **Quality**: 4K, high detail, shallow depth of field on key subjects
- **Avoid**: Overly posed stock-photo looks, visible watermarks, text/logos, single-ethnicity groups
