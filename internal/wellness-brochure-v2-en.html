10-Day Flow

A Premium Retreat Rhythm

Inspired by high-end retreat design, the itinerary balances morning practice, curated cultural immersion, rest windows, and light evening integration. Final routing is confirmed after guest preferences, season, weather, and supplier availability are reviewed.

Day 1
Arrival and Jingdezhen Transfer
Arrive via Shanghai or Hangzhou, then continue to Jingdezhen, Jiangxi. Check in, enjoy welcome tea, and preview the porcelain-making process so the first crafted works can enter the firing timeline early.
Day 2
Jingdezhen Porcelain Making
Work with clay, vessel forms, and artisan guidance in Jingdezhen. Starting porcelain first allows time for finishing, firing, and travel packing before departure.
Day 3
Meditation and Calligraphy
Begin with breathwork and seated meditation. A calligraphy mentor introduces brush, ink, posture, and rhythm, turning Chinese characters into a meditative practice.
Days 4-5
West Lake, Ink Painting and Water Town
Move from classical West Lake ambience into Chinese ink painting, then explore canal lanes, stone bridges, courtyards, and old-town textures.
Days 6-7
Craft Brewery and Gentle Integration
Visit a boutique brewery to understand contemporary craft culture in China, then return to gentle movement, tea, and cultural deepening.
Days 8-9
Artwork Review, Closing Circle and Farewell Dinner
Morning meditation, personal time, optional cultural deepening, review of ceramics, calligraphy, and ink works, and a warm closing dinner.
Day 10
Departure with Personal Works
Departure coordination, protective packing, handover of personal porcelain, calligraphy, and ink paintings, and support for onward travel questions.

Daily Detail

Visits and Experiences

The descriptions below are written for client-facing use. They explain why each visit matters, what guests do on site, and how the day feels.

Day 1

Jingdezhen Arrival and Porcelain Studio Orientation

Guests arrive via Shanghai or Hangzhou and continue to Jingdezhen, Jiangxi. The first program moment introduces clay, vessel forms, porcelain culture, and the firing timeline, so personal works can begin early enough to be finished and packed by departure.

Meals: welcome tea / light dinner subject to arrival time. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay in or near Jingdezhen.
Day 2

Jingdezhen Porcelain Shaping, Painting, and Kiln Handoff

In a guided studio session, guests work with clay, hand pressure, vessel proportion, and accessible blue-and-white painting motifs. Selected works are handed to the studio for firing, finishing, and protective packing.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay in or near Jingdezhen.

Daily Detail

Practice, Brushwork and Landscape

After the Jingdezhen studio work enters the firing process, the journey turns toward meditation, calligraphy, ink painting, and slow scenic discovery.

Day 3

Seated Meditation and Calligraphy

The morning introduces breath, posture, and quiet attention. In the calligraphy session, guests learn the relationship between brush pressure, ink flow, body alignment, and rhythm, then keep their own calligraphy work as part of the final take-home set.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Day 4

West Lake Ambience and Chinese Ink Painting

A slow scenic excursion introduces the classical mood of water, bridges, gardens, and layered views. The afternoon ink painting workshop translates that atmosphere into brushwork, empty space, tonal gradation, and mountain-water composition.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Day 5

Water Town Ancient Lanes and Canal Walk

Guests explore a Chinese water-town setting with canals, stone bridges, courtyards, local textures, and small shops. The visit is paced for observation and photography rather than rushed sightseeing.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Images are for reference only Final on-site arrangements may vary
Daily Detail

Scenery, Craft, and Contemporary Taste

The second half of the program adds contrast: water-town atmosphere, modern craft culture, spacious rest, and a thoughtful close.

Day 6

Boutique Brewery Visit and Craft Beer Tasting

A guided brewery visit introduces contemporary Chinese craft culture: ingredients, fermentation, production flow, and tasting language. Non-alcoholic alternatives can be arranged so the activity remains inclusive and relaxed.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, tasting/light dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Day 7

Gentle Movement and Cultural Deepening

Morning meditation and light movement help the group integrate the travel rhythm. The afternoon can be used for tea, quiet rest, an additional art session, or a light local visit depending on guest energy.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Day 8

Artwork Review and Elegant Rest

Guests review ceramics status, calligraphy, and ink paintings with the team. This day keeps enough rest space for a premium rhythm while making sure personal works are ready for final handover.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.

Daily Detail

Closing and Take-Home Works

The final days leave room for reflection, farewell hospitality, protective packing, and departure with personal works.

Day 9

Integration, Closing Circle, and Farewell Dinner

The final full day is calm and ceremonial. Guests share reflections, enjoy personal time, and gather for a farewell dinner that closes the retreat without rushing the emotional transition.

Meals: breakfast, lunch, farewell dinner. Stay: premium hotel or boutique retreat stay.
Day 10-11

Departure with Personal Works

The team assists with check-out, transfer, rail or airport timing, luggage movement, and onward travel questions. Guests leave with protected porcelain pieces, rolled calligraphy, and ink paintings; for long-haul flights, arrival home may be the following calendar day.

Meals: breakfast on Day 10 where hotel timing allows. Day 11 is an international flight buffer.
