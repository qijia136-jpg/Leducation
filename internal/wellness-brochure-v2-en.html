10-Day Flow

A Premium Retreat Rhythm

Inspired by high-end retreat design, the itinerary balances morning practice, curated cultural immersion, rest windows, and light evening integration. Final routing is confirmed after guest preferences, season, and supplier availability are reviewed.

Day 1
Arrival and Private Check-In
Shanghai or Zhejiang arrival support, welcome tea, retreat orientation, preference review, and quiet evening recovery.
Day 2
Meditation and Calligraphy
Morning breathwork and seated meditation, followed by calligraphy practice, tea, journaling, and a relaxed welcome dinner.
Day 3
West Lake Ambience and Ink Painting
A slow scenic walk, Chinese ink painting, garden lunch, optional rest or massage block, and evening reflection.
Days 4-5
Porcelain Making and Painting
Hands-on porcelain craft, painting on porcelain, tea, quiet focus practice, and a small group art-sharing session.
Days 6-7
Water Town and Craft Brewery
A gentle walk through a China water town, local dining, a boutique brewery visit, relaxed craft beer tasting, and non-alcoholic alternatives on request.
Days 8-9
Integration and Elegant Rest
Morning meditation, light movement, personal time, optional cultural deepening, and a closing circle.
Day 10
Departure and Follow-Up
Departure coordination, feedback collection, and support for onward travel questions.
