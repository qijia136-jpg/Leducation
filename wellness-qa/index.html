Who This Retreat Is For

This adult program is positioned for travelers who prefer a slower rhythm than a standard sightseeing tour: culture, wellness practices, local food, light movement, guided visits, and enough structure to feel looked after.

Best fitAdults seeking cultural immersion with wellness-oriented pacing.

10-Day Journey Framework

Use this as the customer-facing structure until the team confirms the final dated itinerary.

Day 1

Arrival and Orientation

Airport or city arrival support where available, welcome briefing, safety notes, schedule overview, and rest after travel.

Days 2-3

Culture and Gentle Rhythm

Guided cultural visits, tea or local craft learning, neighborhood walks, and light wellness activities suited to adult travelers.

Days 4-6

Immersive Wellness Practices

Slow-paced sessions such as mindful movement, breathing, food culture, traditional lifestyle learning, and reflective time.

Days 7-9

Local Life and Signature Experiences

Deeper cultural immersion, hosted activities, scenic or heritage visits, group sharing, and optional personal time when available.

Day 10

Departure and Follow-Up

Departure coordination, feedback collection, and support for onward travel questions.

Readiness Checklist

Internal staff can also use this list to screen whether a traveler should continue to manual consultation.

Travel Profile

  • Adult traveler or adult group
  • Comfortable with guided group rhythm
  • Interested in culture, local life, and restorative pacing

Must Confirm

  • Current passport and China entry requirements
  • Preferred dates, room type, and budget range
  • Dietary restrictions, allergies, mobility limits, health notes

Escalate to Human

  • Medical concerns or accessibility needs
  • Custom itinerary or private group request
  • Refund, invoice, insurance, visa, or payment questions
