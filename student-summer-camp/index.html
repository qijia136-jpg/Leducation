Five Road Trip Route Options

Each route keeps the same AI + Robotics academic spine. Friday-Saturday road trips can combine university visits, innovation exposure, and scenic learning.

Xi'an

Xi'an Jiaotong + Terracotta Warriors

Visit Xi'an Jiaotong University for engineering inspiration, then explore the Terracotta Warriors and historic city culture.

Shanghai

Fudan, SJTU, Suzhou, Wuxi

Explore Shanghai city views, visit Fudan University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, with Suzhou and Wuxi road trip options.

Hangzhou

Zhejiang University + Westlake University

Visit Zhejiang University and Westlake University, then explore West Lake and Xitang water town.

Xiamen

Xiamen University + Coastal Scenic Sites

Visit Xiamen University and nearby famous scenic spots reachable within a few hours by car, based on season and logistics.

Beijing

Tsinghua, Peking University, Great Wall

Visit Tsinghua and Peking University, then explore the Great Wall, Old Summer Palace, and other major Beijing landmarks.

Robotics Competition and Final Showcase

The final Monday is not just a ceremony. It validates learning through robot performance, design explanation, teamwork, and communication.

Robot Challenge

Teams compete in a mission such as navigation, delivery, line tracking, obstacle avoidance, or a route-specific field challenge.

Engineering Review

Judges review notebooks, iteration choices, debugging notes, responsible AI thinking, and team role distribution.

Final Showcase

Students present their robot, demo video, road-trip insight, and improvement plan to peers, mentors, and invited guests.

Closing Dinner

Certificates, mentor comments, photo moments, family-ready highlights, and farewell dinner.
